# Introduction

>“neurodiversity refers to the natural variations in brain function that exist across the entire population, encompassing both neurotypical and neurodivergent individuals.” (Vargas-Salas et al., 2025, p. 84)

>“the term neurodivergent is used to describe individuals whose neurological characteristics differ significantly from what is considered the predominant or typical cognitive pattern within a given society or context” (Vargas-Salas et al., 2025, p. 84)
