>“Cognitive load is the amount of working memory resources that are used”
>
>“According to Sweller, there are three types of cognitive loads (Sweller, 2016): 
>● Intrinsic. It corresponds to the complexity of the acquired knowledge, without a reference to how it is presented and acquired; 
>● Extrinsic. It defines how information is presented and acquired; 
>● Germane. It defines knowledge integration in long-term memory.” (Souchet et al., 2022, p. 802)
